Subjects

  • Introduction to Computer Science: Basics of computer science, programming, and algorithms.
  • Data Structures and Algorithms: In-depth study of data structures, algorithm design, and analysis.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Principles and applications of AI, machine learning, and neural networks.
  • Database Management Systems: Design, implementation, and management of databases.
  • Cybersecurity: Fundamentals of network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking.
