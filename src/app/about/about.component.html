Adhyantha College of Engineering

Adhyantha College of Engineering, established in 1998, has grown to be a premier institution in the field of engineering education. Founded with a vision to provide quality technical education, the college has consistently evolved with time, adapting to the latest advancements in technology and pedagogy. The campus, spread over 50 acres, boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and spacious lecture halls.

Dr. Suresh Kumar, the current principal, has been instrumental in steering the college towards academic excellence. Under his leadership, the college has received numerous accolades and recognitions, both at the national and international levels.

The college is home to several departments, each led by a highly qualified Head of Department (HOD). About Awards and Achievements

Adhyantha College of Engineering has garnered numerous achievements and awards since its establishment. The college has been consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions in the country by various national ranking agencies. It received the prestigious "Excellence in Engineering Education" award from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for its outstanding academic programs and innovative teaching methods.

The college's research and development wing has been recognized with several grants and accolades, including the "Best Research Institution" award from the Department of Science and Technology (DST). Adhyantha College's students have excelled in national and international competitions, winning awards in robotics, coding, and project design.

The faculty members have also been honored with awards for their contributions to research and education, including the "Best Professor in Engineering" award. The college's commitment to social responsibility has been acknowledged with the "Community Service Excellence" award for its various outreach programs and initiatives.

These accolades reflect Adhyantha College's dedication to academic excellence, research innovation, and community engagement.

Placements

Adhyantha College of Engineering boasts an impressive placement record, with top multinational companies recruiting its graduates every year.
